The Book of Acts

Explore the birth and growth of the early church in our series on the Book of Acts. From the ascension of Jesus to the spread of the Gospel, discover the powerful work of the Holy Spirit through the apostles. Join us as we dive into these foundational stories and learn how they apply to our lives today.

Recent Sermon Series

The Book of Nehemiah

Join us as we dive into the Book of Nehemiah, exploring themes of leadership, faith, and perseverance. This sermon series unpacks Nehemiah’s journey to rebuild Jerusalem’s walls, offering powerful lessons on trusting God and working together in the face of challenges. Discover how Nehemiah’s story inspires us to lead with purpose and rely on God’s strength in every season.
